About Us

Let's Learn With Us!

A huge part of this website is dedicated to recommend swimming class for people. Our basic swimming techniques are intended to familiarise beginners with the water while also teaching them how to stay afloat with minimal effort. Experienced swimmers who want to improve their swimming technique may also want to practise the series of swimming drills specific to each swimming stroke.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide an atmosphere that inspires swimmers to achieve their maximum potential through a strong work ethic, discipline, self-motivation and self-esteem and to instill a life long love of swimming through enjoyment and accomplishment at all levels of swimming.

Vision: To instill a life-long love of swimming and to build a program of excellence in training, team unity, character development and family support where every swimmer has the opportunity to achieve their potential and compete at the highest level.
